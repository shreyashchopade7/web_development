Realme 8 (4G) is the name of my device, currently running on Android 12, based on Realme UI 3.0 link.
It was one of the best device out there under 15k.
Its been one and a half year since I purchased this device.

Its specifications are :

  • Processor: Mediatek Helio G95
  • Display: 60 Hz, 1000 nits (peak), Super AMOLED (8-bit)
  • Camera: 64 MP main, 8 MP ultrawide, 16 MP selfie
  • Battery: 5000 mah with 33 W fast charge
  • Indisplay fingerprint scanner
